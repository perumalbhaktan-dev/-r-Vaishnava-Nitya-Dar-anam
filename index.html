Śrī Vaishnava Nitya Darśanam

Daily Vision of the Divine

In today's fast-moving world — where distractions multiply every moment and the ancient is so easily forgotten — it becomes all the more essential that we know who we are, where we come from, and to Whom we truly belong.

Śrī Vaishnavism is not merely a religion. It is a way of being, a way of seeing, and a way of returning to Śrīman Nārāyaṇa.

This repository documents the daily practices, teachings, and wisdom of the Śrī Vaishnava tradition.
